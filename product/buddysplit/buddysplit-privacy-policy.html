Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how BuddySplit protects your data.

Last updated: June 22, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to BuddySplit's Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy describes how Malih Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our BuddySplit mobile application (the "Service"). This policy applies to all users of our bill-splitting application.

By using BuddySplit, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this Privacy Policy, please do not use our Service.

Quick Overview

BuddySplit is a bill-splitting application that helps you manage shared expenses with friends and groups. When you add expenses or join groups, that information is shared with relevant participants. You control who you share expenses with and what expenses you create. We use Firebase (Google) services to store your data securely, and your data may be processed in the United States.

Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

  • Account means a unique account created for you to access our Service.
  • Application refers to BuddySplit, the mobile software program provided by Malih Solutions.
  • Company (referred to as "the Company," "we," "us," or "our") refers to Malih Solutions.
  • Device means any device that can access the Service, such as a smartphone or tablet.
  • Personal Data is any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
  • Service refers to the BuddySplit Application.
  • You means the individual accessing or using the Service.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Registration Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, display name, and account preferences
  • Expense Data: Expense descriptions, amounts, categories, dates, and notes
  • Group Information: Group names, member lists, and group settings
  • Payment Records: Payment amounts, dates, and settlement information
  • Receipt Images: Photos of receipts uploaded to expenses
  • Comments and Notes: Messages, comments, and notes added to expenses
  • Contact Information: When you add friends, we store their contact information

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, IP address
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, time spent, crash reports
  • Analytics Data: App performance, user behavior patterns, and feature usage statistics

Information from Device Permissions

With your permission, we may access:

  • Camera: To take photos of receipts and profile pictures
  • Photo Library: To select images from your device
  • Contacts: To help you find friends who use BuddySplit (contact information is only stored if you add them as friends)
  • Notifications: To send you push notifications about expenses and group activity

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information about you:

  • Firebase (Google) - For user authentication, data storage, analytics, and crash reporting. Data is processed in the United States. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In - For authentication via Google accounts. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store - For subscription payments and app distribution. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Kingfisher - For image downloading and caching (open-source library)
  • CodeScanner - For QR code scanning to join groups and add friends (open-source library)
  • SwiftyCrop - For local image cropping functionality (open-source library)

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Provide the Service: To enable bill-splitting, expense tracking, and group management
  • Account Management: To create and maintain your account
  • Communication: To send you important service updates and push notifications
  • Security: To protect against fraud and ensure account security
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our Service
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

How We Share Your Information

Within the App

  • With Friends: When you add expenses with friends, they can see the expense details, amounts, and your participation
  • With Group Members: All group members can view expenses, payments, and member information within that group
  • Receipt Images: Receipt photos are visible to all participants in the related expense
  • Payment Information: Settlement records are shared with relevant parties involved in the transaction

With Third Parties

  • Service Providers: With Firebase/Google for data storage and analytics
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale, or acquisition of our business
  • With Your Consent: Any other sharing with your explicit permission

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your personal profile information is permanently deleted
  • Your name in shared expenses and groups is changed to "Former Member"
  • Historical expense data remains visible to other participants but without your identifiable information
  • Receipt images you uploaded are deleted
  • Complete deletion may take up to 30 days to process across all systems

International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our service providers (including Firebase/Google) operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Adequate security measures to protect your data
  • Compliance with applicable data protection laws
