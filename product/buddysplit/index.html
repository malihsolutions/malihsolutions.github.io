Welcome to BuddySplit!

Your ultimate companion for effortlessly managing shared expenses with friends and groups. Simplify financial interactions and keep track of who owes what, making group finances transparent and stress-free.

Key Features

  • Seamless Authentication: Get started quickly with flexible sign-up options including Apple Sign-In, Google Sign-In, or traditional email and password registration.
  • Dynamic Group Management: Easily create new groups for any occasion—from roommates to travel buddies—or join existing ones with friends.
  • Flexible Expense Splitting: Add expenses and split them exactly how you need: Equally, Unequally, by Percentage, or by Share Amount. Categorize expenses for better organization.
  • Clear Balance Overviews: The intuitive "Balances" tab provides a comprehensive financial snapshot, showing how much each person owes in total and offering a detailed breakdown of individual debts.
  • Streamlined Payment Tracking: The "Payments" tab keeps a meticulous record of all transactions within a group. Effortlessly filter payments to view "All," "You Paid," or "Paid to You."
  • Insightful Spending Totals: Gain valuable insights with the "Totals" feature, displaying overall group expenditure, your total contributions, and your accumulated shares.
  • Effortless Member Invitation: Expand your groups with ease by sharing a unique invite link or a simple QR code scan.
